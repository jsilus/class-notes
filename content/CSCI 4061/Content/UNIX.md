## What is UNIX?
- Highly Popular OS
- Many variants
- Principles:
	- Multiprogramming
	- Flexibility
	- Extensibility

## UNIX Evolution
- 1965: MULTICS - MULTiplexed Information and Computing Service
- 1969: UNIX  - Developed at Bell Labs
	- Developed in C
	- Architecture-independent
- Several Evolution Paths:
	- 1983: AT&T System V -> SCO Unix
	- 1980: BSD -> FreeBSD, NetBSD
	- 1991: Linux -> Android
	- macOS
